SEECRET Smart Film

The Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystals film (PDLC film) consists of a polymer matrix of liquid crystals enclosed by an electroconductive PET film coated with indium and tin oxide. The PDLC film is further connected to a conductive copper strip, which is connected to a transformer.

Main Features

  • Privacy protection and space divider: The conversion of the opaque mode into transparent creates more space and serves as a creative tool for architects and builders.
  • Rear projection screen: An excellent solution for a high-resolution rear projection screen. Your showcase can become an advertising space.
  • Damage-proof: If the glass gets damaged, its pieces aren’t thrown in all the directions, but stay in place.
  • Heat Insulation: SEECRET Smart Film blocks solar radiation: 99% of the ultraviolet radiation, 98% of the infrared radiation. It improves the ecological balance in your house and protects the skin. By the way, with Smart Film, the room is cooler in summer and warmer in winter.
  • Sound Insulation: Blocks 20% more of exterior noises compared to glass alone.
  • Multifunctional control: Several types of commands can be chosen and installed.

TECHNOLOGY

The Smart Film is a liquid crystal functional display film that uses polymer dispersed liquid crystals (PDLC) as a structure.

PRINCIPLE

When the film is powered by electricity, the liquid crystals line up, and the window becomes instantly transparent. When the power is off, the liquid crystals return to their normal dispersed position, making the glass opaque.
