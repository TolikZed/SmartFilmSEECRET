Smart Film SEECRET

Product Introduction

SEECRET Smart Film is a product that sticks to glass. Its operating principle is to make windows transparent when lit and opaque when turned off.

The film has multiple fields of application: protection of confidentiality, projection screen, glass protection against damage, heat and sound insulation.

The installation of this smart film is very easy. Just install it on any glass surface!

SEECRET Smart Film Technical Characteristics

Privacy

You choose the space that will be visible.

Interactive Projection

Use an additional screen for home cinema, presentation projection, or as advertising space.

Protection

The film is shockproof and effectively prevents the dispersion of glass fragments.

Soundproofing

Heat Insulation

SEECRET Smart Film

The Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystals film (PDLC film) consists of a polymer matrix of liquid crystals enclosed by an electroconductive PET film coated with indium and tin oxide. The PDLC film is further connected to a conductive copper strip, which is connected to a transformer.
